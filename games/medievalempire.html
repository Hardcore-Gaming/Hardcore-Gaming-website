Medieval Empire

*GAMEDESCRIPTION* Medieval world where you can build a kingdom. start in a area of a map and look for resource build a camp. find other player or npc and invite them to join you. travel look for bandit or rob inncent people take their loot to upgrade your camp to a house. build a townhall to start a settlement recruit more people and build more buildings. make alliances fight bigger targets or start a more settlements and you have a kingdom.
